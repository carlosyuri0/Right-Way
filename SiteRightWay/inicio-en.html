ENEM

What is it? What is it for?

The National High School Examination is today the main gateway to higher education in the country. Brazil. The test enables access to public and private educational institutions (being criteria for winning scholarships and student funding from the federal government). O result is also used for direct admission to universities that use the assessment of integral form or as a complement to their own entrance exams for entry into their courses of graduation. For having replaced the traditional entrance exam in thousands of colleges and universities within the country and abroad - the Enem result is also accepted at universities Portuguese - has become part of the school calendar for students who are completing high school and other Brazilians who wish to enter higher education.

When was it created?

The first Enem test was applied in 1998 with the aim of evaluating students who completed the last stage of basic training. Over the years some changes have taken place. in the application of the exam and, currently, it is the most important and awaited test by students Brazilians who wish to enter higher education.

Who can participate?

Despite being an important test and already known by many, some people still have doubts about it. regarding who can take the Enem. Anyone who has completed high school can participate. of the exam, as well as students who are about to complete it.

Is it possible to obtain a high school diploma with Enem?

No. Until 2016, Enem was also used to obtain high school diplomas, however, as of the 2017 edition, the exam no longer has this purpose. Young people interested in obtaining the certification will have to take the National Examination of Youth and Adult Skills Certification. Learn more about the Encceja exam for high school.
